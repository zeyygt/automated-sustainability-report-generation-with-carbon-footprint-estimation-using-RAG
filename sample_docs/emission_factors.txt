Emission Factors Used in This Report
=====================================

The following emission factors are applied for all carbon footprint calculations
in this sustainability report:

  - Electricity emission factor: 0.99 kgCO2/kWh
  - Natural gas emission factor: 3.50 kgCO2/m3

These values reflect the regional grid emission intensity for Istanbul
as published by the national energy authority for the 2024 reporting period.
